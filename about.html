About Sound Laureatte.

Soundlaureatte is a music website and platform that has millions of original music, mixtape music videos, photographs of musical contents and more. It's a place were musicians from every genre of music, Disc jockeys(DJs), Producers, Video directors and every Music Industry stakeholders can upload and showcase their content to the world. Soundlaureatte has supported lots of musical icons and upcoming artists to say the least. This platform is to reach millions of music and entertainment lovers globally, and to inspire their lives positively.

Facebook & Instagram

When you give users on two of the most popular social media platforms permission to use your songs in their uploaded content, you can earn revenue from it. Distribute with Soundlaureatte, then register your songs at no cost.

Youtube

With Soundlaureatte's YouTube Sound Recording service, you can earn revenue any time your songs are used in videos across YouTube. For a one-time fee, you can register unlimited tracks and YouTube's Content ID system will find any ad revenue owed to you.
